Step 3

Explainable AI Insights

Understand why the model made its decision through multiple visual explanation techniques.

Per-Beat Arrhythmia Probability — Each bar shows the model's confidence that the corresponding beat is an arrhythmia. Red bars exceed the decision threshold.
Normal vs Arrhythmia Waveforms — Overlay of multiple detected beats. The white line shows the mean waveform shape for each class.
Grad-CAM (Last Conv1D Layer) — The red overlay highlights time regions the model focused on most when making its decision. High activation = high influence on classification.
This plot is only available for convolutional model architectures.
Grad-CAM (Second-Last Conv1D Layer) — Sharper, earlier feature activations from a deeper layer of the network.
Not available — model may have only one convolutional layer.
Integrated Gradients — Shows the contribution of each time step to the final prediction. Green = pushes toward arrhythmia confidence  |  Red = pushes toward normal.
